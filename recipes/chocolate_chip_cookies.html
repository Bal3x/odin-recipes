Chocolate Chip Cookies!

Description:

Cream the butter and the sugar. Combine dry ingredients together. Add vanilla and eggs to cream butter. Mix in dry ingredients. Add chocolate chips by hand. Bake!

Ingredients

  • Chocolate Chips
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cream the butter and the sugar.
  2. Combine dry ingredients.
  3. Add Vanilla and Eggs to creamed butter.
  4. Mix dry ingredients.
  5. Add Chocolate Chips by hand.
  6. Bake!

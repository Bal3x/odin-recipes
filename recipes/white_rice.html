White Rice

Description

The best white rice recipe that will make Uncle Roger proud!

Ingredients

  • White Rice
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Wash White Rice
  2. Add White Rice, Water, Salt and Olive Oil to pot.
  3. Bring to a rolling boil.
  4. Lower heat to low, cover pot for 15 minutes.
  5. After 15 minutes, turn off heat and uncover. Let it stand uncovered for 5 minutes.
  6. Use a fork to fluff the Rice.
  7. Serve and Enjoy!
